About Winx

Builders who sweat the detail.

We're a Sydney commercial fitout team that earns trust the slow way — honest quotes, tight programs and finishes that hold up.

Our story

We started Winx to do commercial fitouts properly — and never stopped.

Winx Carpentry grew out of a simple frustration: too many fitouts run late, over budget, and finished to a standard that doesn't last. We set out to be the opposite — a specialist team that quotes honestly, plans tightly, and builds with genuine carpentry skill.

Today we deliver retail, office and franchise fitouts right across Sydney. We know the buildings, the strata rules, the landlords and the trades — so your project moves faster and lands cleaner. Single shopfront or multi-site rollout, we manage it end to end and stand behind the result.
