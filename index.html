Who we are

We're a Sydney fitout team that treats every commercial space like it carries our name — because it does.

Winx Carpentry delivers commercial interior fitouts across Sydney for retailers, offices and franchise rollouts. From a single shopfront to a multi-site program, we manage the build end to end — joinery, finishes, fixtures and handover.
